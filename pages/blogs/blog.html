Chocolate Pound Cake

Pan: 24×7 cm | Temp: 160°C | Time: 25 mins

Ingredients

  • Eggs (M/MS): 2 (~100 g)
  • White Sugar: 70 g
  • Brown Sugar: 30 g
  • Butter (softened): 85 g
  • Cake Flour (薄力粉): 75 g
  • Cocoa Powder: 10 g
  • Baking Powder: ½ tsp (optional)
  • Vanilla Extract: ½ tsp (optional)
  • Salt: tiny pinch

Method

  1. Prep: Line your 24×7 cm loaf pan with baking paper.
  2. Whip: Whisk eggs and both sugars until pale, thick, and foamy (ribbon stage).
  3. Sift: Combine flour, cocoa, baking powder, and salt. Sift together.
  4. Fold: Gently fold the dry mix into the egg-sugar foam with a spatula.
  5. Add Butter: Gently melt butter, cool slightly, then fold carefully into the batter.
  6. Pour: Fill pan to about halfway and smooth the top.
  7. Bake: In a 160°C toaster oven for 20 minutes in 10 minute intervals while rotating the pan each time. Then, for another 5 minutes Tent with foil if browning too quickly.
  8. Check Doneness: A skewer should come out with a few moist crumbs.
  9. Cool: Let sit in pan for 5 min, then cool completely on a rack.

Notes & Tips

  • Egg whip is key: Longer whipping creates a lighter loaf.
  • Folding technique: Fold, don’t stir, to preserve air bubbles.
  • Toaster Oven: Rotate pan halfway if your oven heats unevenly.
  • Batter texture: Should be glossy, thick, but still pourable.
